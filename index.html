I am a Generative AI developer and frontend engineer in my third year of B.Tech study at Adhunik College of Engineering, Ghaziabad, affiliated with AKTU. My practice sits between two adjacent things: the craft of building interfaces that respond, and the engineering required to make language models do something useful inside them.

I founded ResumeAI — a tool for AI-driven resume analysis and optimization — and NexusAI, a prompt-driven image generation application. Both projects are ongoing. Both taught me that prompt engineering is not a trick but a discipline of reading what a model is actually doing.

I write production frontend in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript; I think in component systems; I have come to prefer interfaces that do less, more quietly. The tools I reach for most often are VS Code, Git, and a small set of AI coding assistants I have learned to trust and distrust in equal measure.

"The most useful skill I have learned in three years of building is the willingness to delete code I was proud of yesterday."

— V.K., Notebook, Dec 2025
